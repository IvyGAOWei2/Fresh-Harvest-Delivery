About Us

Welcome to Fresh Harvest Delivery, your one-stop online destination for fresh and high-quality fruit and vegetables delivered straight to your doorstep. At Fresh Harvest Delivery, we are dedicated to revolutionizing your shopping experience with our upgraded Produce and Order Management System.

Our user-friendly webside showcases a wide range of individual products available for sale, including succulent fruits, crisp vegetables, aromatic fresh herbs, vibrant salads, farm-fresh eggs, and delicious honey. Whether you prefer ordering by bunches, weights, each, half size, punnet, or tray, we've got you covered. You can even create your custom order or opt for our convenient premade boxes in veg, fruit, or mixed varieties, available in large, medium, or small sizes. Our boxes can be purchased as a one-time treat or subscribed to on a weekly, fortnightly, or monthly basis, making your healthy eating habits a seamless part of your routine.

Seasonal availability dictates what produce is available for individual sale and included in our subscription boxes, ensuring you always receive the freshest and finest offerings. Our deliveries span across five depots in Christchurch, Invercargill, Wellington, Hamilton, and Auckland, serving the local metropolitan areas with efficiency and promptness.

Our dedicated staff members ensure that every aspect of your Fresh Harvest Delivery experience is top-notch. From adding new products and managing inventory to handling customer inquiries and processing orders, our team is committed to delivering excellence. Our local managers oversee day-to-day operations, ensuring that each depot runs smoothly and efficiently. They also curate the contents of our premade boxes weekly and manage promotional activities to keep things exciting for our customers.

But we don't stop there! Fresh Harvest Delivery is constantly innovating to enhance your experience. Our system boasts additional features like a points rewards-based system, personalized news updates, and comprehensive reporting tools to provide insights into customer preferences and behavior.

Join us at Fresh Harvest Delivery and experience a new era of convenience, quality, and freshness in every delivery.

Return Policy

Eligibility for Returns:

  • Items eligible for return include:
    • Produce or products that are damaged or spoiled upon delivery.
    • Incorrect items delivered that do not match your order.
    • Items with quality issues that do not meet our standards.
  • Please note that due to the perishable nature of our products, we may not be able to accept returns for items that have been opened or used unless they meet the criteria mentioned above.

Initiating a Return:

  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team within 3 days of receiving your order. You can reach us via contact@freshharvestdelivery.co.nz.
  • Please provide your order number, details of the item(s) you wish to return, and a brief description of the issue.

Return Process:

  • Once your return request is approved, we will arrange for the pickup of the item(s) at a convenient time.
  • Please ensure that the item(s) are securely packaged for return to prevent any further damage during transit.
  • Upon receiving the returned item(s), we will inspect them to verify eligibility for a refund or replacement.

Refund or Replacement:

  • If your return is approved, you may choose between a refund to your original payment method or a replacement of the item(s) with a fresh delivery.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the return is received and approved.

Exceptions and Special Cases:

  • In certain circumstances, such as seasonal availability or limited stock, we may offer alternative solutions or credits towards future purchases.
  • Gift card purchases are non-refundable but can be used towards any future orders.

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee:

We value your satisfaction and strive to make every experience with Fresh Harvest Delivery exceptional. If you have any feedback or concerns regarding your order, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to assist you and ensure your complete satisfaction.
